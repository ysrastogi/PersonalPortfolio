Technical Breakdown & Capabilities

NLP Models Used

  • BERT: A state-of-the-art model for classifying user intent (e.g., "Purchase Intent," "Demo Request").
  • VADER: A lexicon-based tool for sentiment analysis, determining if the lead's tone is positive, neutral, or negative.

Scoring Logic (XGBoost)

An XGBoost model combines features from the NLP pipeline with other lead data to produce a final, weighted score.

Feature Engineering

  • Sentiment Score (VADER)
  • Intent Category (BERT Output)
  • Lead Source (Web, Email, etc.)
  • Time of Day / Day of Week
  • Historical Engagement Metrics

Model Performance Snapshot

  • XGBoost Accuracy: 92%
  • Precision on Hot Leads: 89%
  • Intent Classification F1: 90%

Real-World Application & Benefits

Automated Prioritization

Automatically flags "hot" leads for immediate follow-up by the sales team.

Sentiment-Based Routing

Routes leads with negative sentiment to customer retention or support teams to address issues proactively.

Optimized Workflows

Reduces manual effort for sales teams by automatically categorizing and enriching lead profiles.
